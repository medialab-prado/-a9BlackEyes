About

We are a group of artists and data privacy activists. The 'this is you' project looks at how the location data shared with apps like Instagram can compromise users' privacy in ways that they perhaps don't expect, by revealing exactly where they were and often, the precise moment that they were there. Crucially, these posts don't only affect the individual Instagram user's privacy - they also affect that of all the other people shown in their photos and videos.

The aim is to raise awareness of location data on Instagram as a significant privacy issue and to share tips on how people can better control what they are sharing about themselves online while using this app.
